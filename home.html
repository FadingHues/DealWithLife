Are you Suffering from Depression?


Depression is NORMAL! We all are same in one way or other.

Well, to be honest, that is completely normal. No one is perfect, we need to accept that. We can't waste our life thinking about the things that we couldn't achieve. Instead, we should look forward for the things that we can achieve.
